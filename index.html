About Me

Who am i?

I'm Bikal Pudasaini, a Ph.D. Candidate, Power Systems Engineer and Graduate Research Assistant

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Washington State University in Pullman, Washington, working with Dr. Mani Venkatasubramanian. My research focuses on Power Systems Stability and Control. I work on developing applications using PMU data for power systems oscillations monitoring. Currently, I am working on developing a tool to locate the source of forced oscillations in power system using PMU measurements. My research interests also include Machine Learning and Deep Learning applications in power systems especially using PMU data.

Beside my academic life, I love reading books, travelling, cooking, and music is a crucial part of my life. I am always happy to talk about research, outdoor activities, new recipes, songs recommendations. Feel free to contact me if you want to collaborate on some projects. I am open to answer any questions regarding my work or anything.
